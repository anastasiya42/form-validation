Form Validation

HTML5 Form Validation

HTML5 form validation is useful because not only does it validate forms to ensure correct information is inputted, but it also eases the burden on Javascript.

It allows for validation to occur on the client side in browsers that support its new features.

JavaScript Form Validation

Javascript gives even more access to ways to validate forms.

Functions can be called at various times such as before or after a form is submitted. These function can test against things such as empty fields, or test against a pattern to ensure information is correct.
